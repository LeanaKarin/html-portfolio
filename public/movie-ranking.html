My Favorite Movies

My top three movies that I can think of for this project

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

This is a mind-bending movie about two people who are in love with eachother but don't remember one-another.

True Romance

A Quintin Tarantino script directed by Tony Scott - when a kung-fu movie buff falls in love with a call girl on her first night on the job. Their romance is tested as they try to make money and run away from their past lives.

There Will be Blood

An Upton Sinclair book adaptation about an oil man and the life he builds around this world. The last 20 minutes of the movie has an epic monologue.
